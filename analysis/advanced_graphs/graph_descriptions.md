# PrepSense Graph Descriptions

## Graph 1: Signal Distribution Comparison
**File:** `signal_distribution.png`

Visualizes the probability distribution of ObservedPrepTime versus ReconstructedPrepTime using combined histogram and KDE plots. Demonstrates that ReconstructedPrepTime has a tighter, more concentrated distribution with reduced variance, indicating superior signal quality and prediction consistency.

---

## Graph 2: Variance Comparison
**File:** `variance_comparison.png`

Bar chart comparing the variance of ObservedPrepTime and ReconstructedPrepTime signals. Shows the percentage reduction in variance achieved by PrepSense, quantifying the improvement in signal stability and reliability for kitchen preparation time predictions.

---

## Graph 3: Prediction Error Comparison
**File:** `prediction_error_comparison.png`

Compares mean prediction errors between baseline system and PrepSense. Demonstrates PrepSense's superior accuracy with lower prediction errors, directly translating to more reliable customer ETAs and improved operational planning.

---

## Graph 4: Rider Idle Time Reduction
**File:** `idle_time_reduction.png`

Bar chart showing average rider idle time for baseline versus PrepSense systems. Quantifies operational efficiency gains by reducing unproductive wait times, leading to improved rider utilization and cost savings.

---

## Graph 5: Time Series Signal Smoothness
**File:** `time_series_signal.png`

Time series visualization comparing raw and smoothed signals of ObservedPrepTime and ReconstructedPrepTime over order sequence. Illustrates that ReconstructedPrepTime exhibits smoother temporal patterns with less noise, enabling better trend analysis and forecasting capabilities.

---

## Graph 6: Confidence Interval Prediction
**File:** `confidence_interval.png`

Shows PrepSense predictions with 95% confidence intervals, demonstrating prediction uncertainty bounds. Enables risk-aware decision making by providing probabilistic estimates rather than point predictions, crucial for operational planning and resource allocation.

---

## Graph 7: Business Impact Projection
**File:** `business_impact.png`

Annual business impact projection showing total rider idle hours for baseline system versus PrepSense at 1 billion orders per year scale. Quantifies massive operational savings in millions of hours, demonstrating significant ROI and scalability of the PrepSense solution.

---

## Graph 8: Kitchen Load vs Prep Time
**File:** `kitchen_load_vs_prep.png`

Advanced technical scatter plot with regression analysis showing relationship between kitchen load (concurrent orders) and preparation time. Reveals how PrepSense accounts for kitchen load dynamics, enabling better capacity planning and load balancing strategies.
